Egg and Rice

Description

Looking for something savoury, sweet, something to hit that spot in your mouth, look no further this easy recipe will give it to you and leave you satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt,Sugar and pepper
  • Royco

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and beat them. Put a pan on low heat and pour the egg inside stiring it to scrumble the egg.
  2. When the eggs are done, chop up 5 tomatos into big chucks along with some onion and garlic.
  3. Put a pan on fire(medium heat ), add oil and then onions and garlic , fry until soft then add tomatoes, soy sauce, teaspoon of sugar , 2 pinches of salt, pepper depending on how hot you like it, cube of royco and lasty water
  4. Mix well and let simmer for a few minutes(7 minutes)
