Lentils and Rice

Description

An easy,delious and cheap recipe to make when you are lazy to cook.

Ingredients

  • lentils
  • 2 Rice
  • 4 tomatoes,onion,2 carrots, 3 cloves of garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Parika, chilli, tumeric, chicken masala, salt sugar
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil the lentils for ten minutes in a cup of salted water.
  2. Chop up the onions, tomatoes, carrots and garlics.
  3. Prep spices depending on you tastes.
  4. Put a pan on medium heat, add oil then fry the onions, carrots and green pepper till soft. Once soft and the tomatoes and make a paste.
  5. Add spice mix and soysauce and mix well
  6. Add the lentils and mix well
  7. Add a little water, mix and let boil for 10 minutes.
  8. Boil 2 cups of water adding rosemary in the rice for favour
